Our Story

Crafts of Pakistan by Arshi celebrates the heritage of handmade artistry. Every piece — whether a basket, mat, or tray — is crafted from natural date palm leaves and straw, using techniques passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

What began as a small family passion has grown into a mission: to preserve Pakistan’s traditional crafts while empowering the hands that make them.
